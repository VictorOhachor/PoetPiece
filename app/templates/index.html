MISSION

My buttocks stayed comfortably in my seat as I recited my new poem. I could see her eyes lit with emotions. These emotions appeared so mixed that I could barely let go of the gaze. My eyes searched peculiarly, analysing every wave of emotion I could perceive.

With more sleuthing comes more responsibilities
— My mind sang me
What is your name?
— She whispered
Call Me A PoetPiece!
— I replied, winking.
Why do you compose poems?
— She asked again with a beckoning smile.
Which of these reasons were brought to life by my poem?
— I asked with an exquisite cackle.
NONE!
— She snorted.
Don't conclude just yet... Click the button below to access more poems for free
— I said with a taunting smile on my face.
